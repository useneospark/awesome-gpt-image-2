## Prompts

### Retro 8-Bit Landscape

> A sprawling 8-bit pixel art landscape of a medieval castle during a thunderstorm. Purple lightning bolts illuminate the sky. A dragon silhouette on the highest tower. Retro video game aesthetic, limited color palette, dithering patterns. NES-era graphics but with modern color theory.

### Knitted Forest World

> A macro shot of a tiny forest made entirely out of colorful knitted wool. Knitted squirrels, birds, and mushrooms. Each tree is a different shade of green yarn. Soft, tactile texture, shallow depth of field. Macro photography style with warm studio lighting. Whimsical, craft aesthetic.

### Double Exposure Portrait

> A double exposure silhouette of a woman's profile. Inside the silhouette is a dense, foggy pine forest with a soaring eagle. White background, artistic fine art photography style. Clean edges, perfectly merged images. Dreamy, symbolic, editorial art piece.

### Pencil Sketch Sports Car

> A detailed charcoal and pencil sketch of a vintage 1960s Porsche 911 on textured cream-colored drawing paper. Realistic shading with smudged graphite edges. Visible paper grain and tooth. Some construction lines still visible. Artist's hand partially in frame holding a pencil. Fine art drawing aesthetic.

### 3D Vinyl Toy Robot

> A cute 3D vinyl toy of a robot holding a small flower. Glossy plastic material in bright pastel colors — mint green body, pink joints, yellow eyes. Soft studio lighting with a clean white background. Slight surface imperfections for realism. Designer toy photography, collectibles aesthetic.

### Surrealist Whale Clouds

> A giant humpback whale floating through a sea of fluffy white clouds above a vast golden desert. The whale's back carries a small, lush green forest with a waterfall. Surrealist oil painting style in the tradition of Magritte and Dali. Vibrant colors, dreamlike lighting, impossible scale.
