# Fantasy & Anime Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for fantasy art, anime, and stylized illustrations.

## Prompts

### Ufotable Warrior Princess

> Epic anime-inspired fantasy warrior princess with flowing silver-white hair that reaches her waist. She wears ornate silver armor with sapphire gemstones. A glowing celestial sword in her right hand, particle effects of light dancing around the blade. Ufotable animation studio production quality. Dynamic pose, wind-swept clothing. 4K, cinematic lighting, particle effects.

### Studio Ghibli Forest Spirit

> Studio Ghibli style painterly scene. A small forest spirit that looks like a glowing white fox with three fluffy tails sits on a moss-covered stone in an ancient forest. Fireflies dance around it. Soft watercolor background with dappled sunlight through leaves. Miyazaki aesthetic, hand-painted feel, warm and nostalgic.

### Arcane Style Character

> Arcane Netflix animated series style illustration. A young woman with blue-tipped braided hair and steampunk goggles pushed up on her forehead. She wears a leather aviator jacket with brass buckles. Purple and teal color palette, Fortiche studio aesthetic. Painterly digital art, dramatic rim lighting, cinematic composition.

### Magic The Gathering Dragon

> Fantasy illustration in the style of a Magic The Gathering card. A colossal red dragon with obsidian scales emerges from molten lava, wings spread wide. A tiny knight in the foreground for scale, holding a shield. Greg Rutkowski influence, dramatic perspective, rich saturated colors. Fantasy card art quality.

### Cyberpunk Samurai

> Cyberpunk fantasy fusion. A female samurai with a chrome katana stands on a rain-slicked rooftop in Neo-Tokyo. Traditional kimono made of holographic fabric. Holographic cherry blossoms fall around her. Neon signs reflect in her visor. Otomo/Katsuhiro style meets Ghost in the Shell. Rain, neon, tradition meets future.

### Pixar-Style Character

> A 3D Pixar-style character of a friendly robot holding a small flower, 3/4 front view. Soft cinematic key light from above, warm rim light from behind. Large expressive eyes, soft round cheeks, gentle smile. Smooth subsurface scattering, fluffy hair with stray strands. Background: clean pastel gradient. Feature-film render quality.

### Dark Fantasy Sorceress

> Dark fantasy dark elf sorceress with long flowing midnight-purple hair, pointed ears, and luminous violet eyes. She wears a constellation-patterned cloak that seems to contain actual stars. Arcane energy swirls around her hands. Star field background. Dramatic lighting from below, creating spooky uplight. Digital painting, high detail.

### Ethereal Mermaid

> Ethereal underwater fantasy. A graceful mermaid with iridescent teal and violet scales swims through a coral reef. Long flowing hair moves with the current. Bioluminescent jellyfish drift nearby. Rays of sunlight pierce the water surface above. Lisa Frank meets National Geographic. Dreamy, vibrant, magical.

### Steampunk Airship Captain

> Illustrated steampunk fantasy portrait. A young female airship captain in a brass-buttoned red military coat with gold epaulets. Goggles rest on her leather aviator cap. A brass mechanical bird perches on her shoulder. Nausicaa/Howl's Moving Castle inspired style. Warm, adventurous, detailed illustration.

### Chibi Character Lineup

> A lineup of 5 cute chibi characters representing different seasons: Spring (pink cherry blossoms), Summer (sun hat and watermelon), Autumn (orange leaves and scarf), Winter (blue coat and snowflakes), and a tiny seasonal spirit. Kawaii style, big heads, small bodies, pastel colors. Clean white background. Character design sheet layout.
