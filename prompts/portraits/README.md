# Portraits & Beauty Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for portrait photography and beauty shots.

## Prompts

### Extreme Close-Up Beauty

> Extreme close-up beauty portrait. A stunning model with wet dewy skin and tousled damp hair. Water droplets on collarbones. Shot with ring light creating perfect circular catchlights in the eyes. Macro lens, f/16 for extreme sharpness on every eyelash. Fenty Beauty campaign aesthetic. Clean, minimal, high-fashion.

### Luxury Editorial Portrait

> Luxury editorial portrait. A gorgeous model wearing an elegant black velvet off-shoulder gown reclines on a dark velvet chaise longue. Baroque Rembrandt lighting from camera-left, deep shadows on the right side of her face. Shot on medium format, 100mm lens at f/2. Visible fabric texture, glossy lip highlight. Vogue Italia aesthetic.

### Environmental Street Portrait

> Environmental street portrait. A 20-something Tokyo local with bleached blonde hair and oversized vintage streetwear stands in front of a Shibuya crossing at dusk. Neon reflections in their eyes. Fujifilm X100 aesthetic, 23mm lens, film simulation color grading. Authentic, documentary style.

### High Fashion Vogue Cover

> High-end fashion portrait in the style of a Vogue Italia cover. A striking model with razor-sharp cheekbones and slicked-back platinum hair. Wearing metallic silver couture with geometric shoulder pieces. Hard studio light creating dramatic shadows. Shot on Hasselblad, 100mm lens. Avant-garde makeup, strong brow, negative space composition.

### Soft Natural Light Portrait

> Soft natural light portrait. A woman with wavy chestnut hair sits by a large north-facing window in a white-walled studio. Morning light creates soft Rembrandt window light on her face. She wears a cream linen shirt. Minimal makeup, authentic expression. Shot on 85mm at f/2, slight overexposure on the window side. Clean, airy, editorial.

### Dramatic Black & White

> Dramatic black and white portrait. A man with a short salt-and-pepper beard and intense dark eyes looks directly at camera. Peter Lindbergh style, split lighting — one side of face fully lit, the other in deep shadow. Shot on medium format film, 80mm lens. Raw, unretouched skin texture. Powerful, masculine energy.

### Dreamy Pastel Beauty

> Dreamy pastel beauty portrait. A model with soft pink lips, dewy skin, and flushed cheeks looks slightly upward. Blush pink backdrop, softbox lighting creating even, cloud-like illumination. Wispy hair strands catch the light. Shot on 100mm macro at f/2.8. Romantic, ethereal, beauty campaign aesthetic.

### Golden Hour Portrait

> Sun-drenched golden hour portrait. A woman in a flowing cream linen dress stands in a wheat field at sunset. 135mm telephoto lens compressing the background into soft golden bokeh. Lens flare from the setting sun. Wind catches her dress and hair. Warm, nostalgic, romantic atmosphere. Shot on full-frame, f/2.

### Dark Academia Portrait

> Dark academia editorial portrait. A young woman with auburn hair in a loose braid wears an oversized wool cardigan over a white Oxford shirt. She sits at a green banker's lamp-lit desk in an old library. Bookshelves tower behind her. Warm tungsten light, shallow depth of field. Shot on 50mm at f/1.4. Intellectual, cozy, moody.

### Corporate Headshot

> Professional corporate headshot. A confident man in his late 30s wearing a charcoal suit and light blue tie. Neutral grey background. Classic three-point lighting setup. Shot on 85mm at f/5.6 for sharp focus throughout. Approachable smile, direct eye contact. Clean, polished, executive-level quality.
