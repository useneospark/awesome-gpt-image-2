# Typography & Posters Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for typography-focused designs and poster art.

## Prompts

### Vintage Signage

> A weathered vintage hand-painted sign on a brick wall. The text "OPEN LATE" in bold retro serif letters, painted in faded cream and red. Rust stains, peeling paint, authentic patina. Shot straight-on, even lighting. Typography-focused, nostalgic Americana aesthetic.

### Neon Sign Typography

> A glowing neon sign against a dark brick wall at night. The text reads "DREAM BIG" in cursive neon tubing, hot pink and electric blue gradient. Slight motion blur on the glow. Reflection on wet pavement below. Urban nightlife aesthetic, cinematic.

### Minimalist Quote Poster

> A minimalist typography poster on a textured off-white paper background. The quote "LESS IS MORE" in thin, elegant Helvetica Neue Light, centered. Below, in tiny sans-serif: "Dieter Rams, 1995." Generous white space. Swiss design aesthetic, museum-quality print.

### Graffiti Typography

> Bold graffiti-style typography on a concrete wall. The word "CREATE" in wildstyle graffiti — intricate arrows, 3D drop shadow, fill pattern of flames in orange and yellow. Chrome outline. Photorealistic wall texture, cracks and stains visible. Street art documentary photography style.

### Japanese Vertical Typography

> Traditional Japanese vertical calligraphy on handmade washi paper. Black sumi ink brushstrokes forming the kanji for "harmony" (和). Red seal stamp (hanko) in the bottom left corner. Subtle paper texture, ink bleed at edges. Zen aesthetic, museum piece quality.

### Diner Menu Board

> Create a photoreal photograph of a 24 hour diner menu board at 5 in the morning, shot from the counter seat at slight angle. Plastic letter tracks, uneven letter spacing, one missing letter slot, yellowed light from incandescent bulbs, legible prices, categories labeled BREAKFAST, GRIDDLE, SANDWICHES, SIDES, DRINKS, and a daily special that reads CHICKEN FRIED STEAK 8.25. The type must be 100 percent readable and physically believable. No watermark, no brand logos, no text artifacts.

### Billboard with Exact Text

> Create a realistic roadside billboard mockup at sunset. Billboard headline (EXACT TEXT, one line only): "Fresh and clean". Typography: Bold sans serif, centered, high contrast, clean kerning, easy to read from a distance. Layout: Bottle on the right, headline on the left, generous negative space. Constraints: Render the text verbatim. No extra words. No duplicate text. No additional logos. No watermark.

### Retro CRT Terminal Screen

> Create a photograph of a 1992-era CRT monitor displaying a bulletin board system terminal. Phosphor green text on black. ASCII banner: THE NIGHT OWL BBS. Main menu items: 1 Message Base 2 File Library 3 Chat Rooms 4 User Config 5 Log Off. Status line at bottom: user handle GHOSTWALKER. Subtle scanline glow, dusty monitor bezel, keyboard slightly out of focus. No modern UI elements. No watermark.

### Extreme Perspective Typography Bridge

> A cross-sea bridge photographed from a dramatic cinematic side angle. Giant bold sans-serif text painted directly onto the bridge surface, starting large near the camera and progressively foreshortening toward the vanishing point. The letterforms conform to the surface curvature, appearing surface-integrated rather than floating. Oversized bright yellow text with sharp orange outline, extreme perspective distortion aligned to the vanishing point. Cinematic lighting, subtle motion blur, poster-grade dynamic integrated typography, modern advertising aesthetics.

### Movie Title Card

> A cinematic movie title card. Deep space background with distant galaxies. The title "EPOCH" in massive, custom-designed futuristic typography — each letter is a geometric structure with glowing edges. Subtitle below: "A Journey Beyond Time." 4K, epic scale, sci-fi blockbuster aesthetic.
