# Cinematic & Film Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for cinematic and film-style image generation.

## Featured Prompts

### Film Noir Femme Fatale

> Film noir cinematic shot. A dangerously beautiful femme fatale in a curve-hugging red silk dress stands in a rain-soaked Hong Kong alley at 2am. Neon signs in teal and crimson reflect on wet cobblestones. She holds a cigarette, smoke curling upward. Shot on 35mm film, 50mm lens at f/1.4, shallow depth of field. Deep shadows, high contrast, moody atmosphere. Cinematic color grading with teal shadows and warm red highlights.

### Wes Anderson Hotel Lobby

> Wes Anderson style cinematic composition. A 1960s hotel concierge in a burgundy uniform stands dead-center in a perfectly symmetrical Art Deco lobby. Pastel pink walls, gold geometric patterns, marble floor with black and white checkerboard pattern. Twin bellhop carts flanking the entrance. Even, flat lighting. Shot on 35mm film with slight vignette. Quirky, meticulous framing.

### Denis Villeneuve Desert Epic

> Epic cinematic wide shot in Denis Villeneuve style. A lone hooded figure in flowing desert robes walks across a vast orange sand dune under a burning sunset. Extreme scale — the figure is tiny against the landscape. Dust particles catch the golden light. Shot on 65mm film, 18mm ultra-wide lens. Natural film grain, no CGI smoothness. Dune (2021) color palette.

### Miami Vice Neon

> 1980s Miami Vice cinematic shot. A woman in a white linen blazer drives a red convertible Ferrari Testarossa at night through downtown Miami. Palm trees and neon motel signs blur past in motion. She wears sunglasses reflecting the pink and turquoise glow of the city. Lens flare, soft film grain. Teal and magenta color grade. Ultra-realistic 4K, anamorphic lens characteristics.

### Italian Giallo Horror

> Cinematic horror frame in the style of a 1970s Italian giallo film. A woman in a white nightgown stands at the end of a long Victorian hallway lit only by flickering red lamplight. Her back is turned to camera. Her shadow stretches impossibly long toward the camera. Wallpaper is blood-red damask. Shallow depth of field, 28mm lens slightly distorted. Grainy film look. Deep red and black color story. Ultra-detailed 4K.

### Studio Ghibli Live Action

> Cinematic still styled as a live-action Studio Ghibli adaptation. A young woman in a simple blue linen dress stands in a vast green hillside field of wildflowers. A gentle breeze moves her hair and dress. Golden hour backlight creating a warm halo. Distant white clouds against a cerulean sky. Shot on medium format film, 80mm lens, painterly color grading reminiscent of Howl's Moving Castle. Dreamlike, nostalgic atmosphere.

### Korean Crime Thriller

> Cinematic still from a modern Korean crime thriller. Two men face each other across a small Seoul apartment kitchen table. One is a detective in a rumpled beige trench coat, the other a suspect in a black turtleneck. Fluorescent tube lighting from above casts harsh shadows. Soju bottles and side dishes on the table. Shot on Arri Alexa, 40mm lens at f/2. Bong Joon-ho inspired framing. Tense, claustrophobic mood.

### French New Wave Cafe

> Black and white French New Wave cinematic still. A young woman in a striped Breton shirt and dark bob haircut smokes a Gauloise at a Paris cafe table. She gazes past the camera with an enigmatic expression. Natural window light from camera-right. Godard aesthetic, 1962. 35mm film grain, slightly overexposed highlights. Intellectual, melancholic atmosphere.

### Jazz Bar Interior

> Moody jazz bar interior. A mysterious woman in a sheer black lace dress sits on a velvet barstool at a mahogany bar. Rembrandt lighting from a single Edison bulb above. Smoke drifts through beams of warm amber light. A saxophone player is silhouetted in the background. Shot on medium format film, 80mm lens at f/2. Deep shadows, rich warm tones. Film noir atmosphere.

### Action Figure Unboxing

> A stylized action figure of a cyberpunk samurai sealed inside a premium plastic blister pack, photographed straight-on. The cardboard backing is glossy with a bold header reading "NEON RONIN" in oversized chrome sans-serif caps and a smaller tagline "Tokyo 2087." The figure is posed with a plasma katana and tech visor. Studio product photography, soft top lighting, clean off-white background, subtle reflection on the floor.

### Retro Terminal Screen

> Create a photograph of a 1992-era CRT monitor displaying a bulletin board system terminal. Phosphor green text on black background. ASCII banner reading "THE NIGHT OWL BBS." Main menu items: 1 Message Base, 2 File Library, 3 Chat Rooms, 4 User Config, 5 Log Off. Status line at bottom: user handle GHOSTWALKER. Subtle scanline glow, dusty monitor bezel, keyboard slightly out of focus. No modern UI elements. No watermark.

### Silent Film Astronaut

> A high-quality 1920s black and white silent film still showing an astronaut in a bulky vintage spacesuit walking through a vintage 1920s New York City street. Film grain, light leaks, authentic antique look. Title card style text overlay: "ONE GIANT LEAP FOR MANKIND." Intertitle cards visible. Keystone Cops-style framing.
