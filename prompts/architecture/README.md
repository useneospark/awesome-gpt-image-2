# Architecture & Interior Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for architectural visualization and interior design.

## Prompts

### Modern Living Room

> Interior design photography of a sunken living room with a large floor-to-ceiling window overlooking a pine forest. Minimalist furniture: a cream-colored boucle sofa, concrete fireplace, and a single statement floor lamp. Soft evening light flooding in. Clean lines, neutral palette, Scandinavian-Japanese fusion aesthetic. Shot on 24mm tilt-shift lens.

### A-Frame Cabin

> A modern A-frame cabin made of charred wood (shou sugi ban) and glass, nestled in a snowy mountain landscape. Warm orange lights glowing from inside. Blue hour photography, high contrast between warm interior and cold exterior. Smoke from the chimney. Shot on 16mm wide lens. Cozy, aspirational, architectural digest quality.

### Luxury Boutique Interior

> A luxury boutique interior with gold accents and marble flooring. Clothing racks spaced out neatly with designer garments. A crystal chandelier centerpiece. Elegant warm lighting. High-end architectural visualization. Shot on 24mm, f/8 for sharpness throughout. Fashion retail design aesthetic.

### Industrial Loft Kitchen

> An industrial loft kitchen with exposed brick walls, steel beam ceiling, and reclaimed wood countertops. Large factory windows let in morning light. Stainless steel appliances, open shelving with ceramic dishware. A single hanging Edison bulb over a concrete island. Warm, lived-in, industrial-chic aesthetic.

### Japanese Ryokan Interior

> Traditional Japanese ryokan room interior. Tatami mats, low wooden table with tea set, shoji screens filtering soft natural light. A small ikebana flower arrangement on a tokonoma alcove. Minimal, serene, warm wood tones. Shot on 35mm at f/2.8. Wabi-sabi aesthetic, peaceful atmosphere.

### Futuristic Museum Hall

> A futuristic museum interior with sweeping curved white walls and dramatic skylights. A massive holographic art installation floats in the center of the grand hall. Reflective polished concrete floor. A few tiny visitors for scale. Zaha Hadid-inspired architecture. Clean, monumental, awe-inspiring.
