# Product Photography Prompts

A curated collection of GPT Image 2 prompts for product photography and e-commerce visuals.

## Prompts

### Minimalist Electric Kettle

> A premium matte white electric kettle on a light oak wooden stand. Simple grey background. Studio lighting with a crisp drop shadow. High-end lifestyle photography. Clean, Scandinavian aesthetic. Shot at f/8, 50mm lens. Product catalog quality.

### Coffee Bag Packaging

> A close-up of a brown kraft paper coffee bag standing upright. The label is perfectly legible and reads "MIDNIGHT ROAST" in bold black vintage typography with "Small Batch | Dark Roast | Notes of Chocolate & Cherry" underneath. Small coffee beans scattered around the base. Realistic paper texture, matte finish. Soft natural light from the left. Shot on 100mm macro at f/4.

### Perfume in Water

> A clear glass perfume bottle filled with light pink liquid, submerged in clear water with small air bubbles rising around it. Dramatic refraction of light through the water and glass. Dark background, single spotlight from above. Luxury advertising style. Shot on macro lens, f/5.6. No text on bottle.

### Wireless Earbuds Macro

> A pair of sleek silver wireless earbuds inside an open matte-black charging case. The case has a small glowing green LED light. Dark metallic background. Macro photography, extreme detail on the metal mesh grilles and silicone tips. Shot on 100mm macro at f/2.8. Tech product advertising aesthetic.

### Skincare Flat Lay

> A flat-lay photograph of a high-end skincare set on a marble surface. Surrounded by pink peony petals and fresh green eucalyptus leaves. Soft natural window light from the top-left. Clean white text on the packaging reads "7-DAY GLOW" in a modern serif font. Minimalist, luxury beauty aesthetic. Shot on 50mm at f/4, overhead angle.

### Mechanical Keyboard

> A premium mechanical keyboard with translucent keycaps and RGB underglow in teal and purple. Floating keycap design, exposed switches. Dark walnut desk surface, warm ambient light. Product photography, 35mm at f/2.8, shallow depth of field on the WASD keys. Gaming peripheral aesthetic.

### Watch Product Shot

> A luxury dive watch with a blue ceramic bezel and stainless steel bracelet, placed on a dark slate surface. Dramatic side lighting creating sharp reflections on the sapphire crystal. Water droplets on the surface. Shot on 100mm macro at f/8. Every detail sharp — the date window, the lume plots, the engraved caseback. Product catalog quality.

### Sneaker Hero Shot

> A single pristine white leather sneaker floating at a 45-degree angle against a gradient background from warm peach to soft lavender. Clean studio lighting with a soft reflection below. The sneaker has subtle perforations and a gum sole. Shot on 50mm at f/8. Minimalist, Instagram-worthy product photography.
