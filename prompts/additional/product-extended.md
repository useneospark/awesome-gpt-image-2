# Product Photography Prompts (Additional)

More GPT Image 2 prompts for product photography and e-commerce visuals.

*Source: gptimage2.github.io, gptimage2ai.com*

---

### Skincare Bottle Hero

> Create a premium skincare product photo of a frosted glass serum bottle on a pale stone platform. Use soft diffused studio lighting, subtle water droplets, botanical shadows on the background, clean negative space, realistic reflections, and an upscale beauty brand look suitable for a landing page hero image.

### Running Shoe Campaign

> Generate a dynamic product photography shot of a lightweight running shoe suspended above a textured track surface. Add small dust particles, crisp side lighting, sharp material detail, a deep blue background, realistic shadow contact, and a polished sports campaign style without adding logos or brand names.

### Desk Lamp Lifestyle

> Create a realistic lifestyle product shot of a minimalist matte black desk lamp on a walnut desk beside a notebook and ceramic mug. Use late afternoon window light, soft shadows, a clean modern home office, shallow depth of field, and enough detail to show the lamp finish clearly.

### Headphone Detail Shot

> Create a close-up commercial product photo of wireless over-ear headphones resting on a brushed metal surface. Emphasize fabric texture, hinge detail, soft rim lighting, premium black and graphite tones, realistic reflections, and a composition that leaves clean space for web copy on the right.

### Reusable Bottle Outdoors

> Generate a bright outdoor product photo of a stainless steel reusable water bottle standing on a sunlit rock near a mountain trail. Use realistic condensation, a slightly blurred landscape background, clean natural colors, crisp product edges, and a practical ecommerce campaign mood.

### Chocolate Packaging Set

> Create a premium product arrangement of three artisan chocolate bars in textured paper sleeves on a marble surface. Use readable but fictional package names, warm side lighting, small cacao nibs as props, careful alignment, realistic packaging folds, and a refined editorial food photography style.

### Product Shot — Sneaker on Water

> A dramatic product photograph of a white sneaker floating above a shallow pool of water. Water droplets frozen mid-splash around the shoe. Gradient studio background transitioning from warm peach to cool blue. Professional product lighting: key light from above, fill light from the left, rim light creating a halo effect. Ultra-sharp detail, commercial advertising quality.

### Flat Lay Food Photography

> An overhead flat-lay food photograph of a brunch spread on a rustic wooden table. Items include: avocado toast with a poached egg, a bowl of açaí topped with granola and berries, a cup of pour-over coffee, fresh orange juice in a glass carafe, scattered linen napkins, and small potted succulents. Natural daylight from a window on the left, soft shadows, warm inviting tones.

### Knolling Tech Accessories

> A knolling-style flat-lay arrangement of tech accessories on a matte black surface. Items arranged in a precise grid: wireless earbuds, USB-C cables, a portable SSD, SD cards, a lens cleaning kit, a compact tripod, and a laptop sleeve. Everything aligned at perfect 90-degree angles with equal spacing. Soft overhead lighting, no harsh shadows, satisfying visual organization.
